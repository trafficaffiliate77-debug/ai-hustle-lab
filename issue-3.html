I built an AI micro-tool in 7 days for $0 — the honest week-1 report

Seven days ago I started building an AI micro-tool website.

Goal: see if I could build and launch a real AI side hustle for $0, using only free tools, and document everything.

Here's the honest week-1 report.

The idea

I'm building an online character counter tool — the kind people search for when writing tweets, meta descriptions, or essays with character limits.

I picked this because:

  • People search "character counter" ~500K times/month
  • The existing top results are ugly and outdated
  • It's buildable in a weekend with AI
  • Monetization is clear (display ads + affiliate links)

What I spent

Total: $0

  • Domain: $0 (using free Cloudflare subdomain for now)
  • Hosting: $0 (Cloudflare Pages free tier)
  • AI tools: $0 (Claude free + ChatGPT free)
  • Design: $0 (Canva free)

Time invested

  • Research & validation: 3 hours
  • Build: 8 hours
  • Content & SEO: 4 hours
  • Total: ~15 hours over 7 days

What I learned

1. Free AI tools are slower but workable. Claude free has wait times during peak hours. Plan content in batches.

2. The hardest part isn't building — it's picking the right idea. I almost shipped 3 bad ideas before settling on this one. Validation matters.

3. SEO is a slow game. I won't know if this works for 60-90 days. Patience is the hardest part.

Results so far (week 1)

Honest numbers:

  • Visitors: 23
  • Signups: 0 (no email list yet)
  • Revenue: $0
  • Google ranking: not in top 100 yet

Yes, it's slow. Yes, this is normal. The compound effect starts around month 3-4.

Next week's plan

Add 5 articles, submit to Google Search Console, apply for AdSense, and start tracking keyword rankings.

The takeaway

Building an AI side hustle for $0 is real. It's not fast, it's not easy, and it's not magic. But it's possible — and the tools in 2026 are good enough that you don't need a budget to start.
