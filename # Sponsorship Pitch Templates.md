## Email Template #1: Initial Pitch

**Subject line options:**
1. Quick question about [BRAND] + AI Hustle Lab

3. [BRAND] readers would love AI Hustle Lab

---

- [X,000] engaged subscribers (mostly solopreneurs, side hustlers, freelancers)

**Sponsorship options:**

📧 **Solo sponsorship** ($[X])
- Your dedicated message to my full list
- Native, conversational tone (not banner ads)
- Includes 1 follow-up in the next issue
- Custom CTA and landing page

📧 **Featured mention** ($[X])
- Brief mention in a regular issue
- Single CTA link
- Lower commitment, high visibility

📧 **Multi-issue package** ($[X])
- 3-4 mentions over 6 weeks
- Consistent presence in inbox
- Better ROI for brands testing the waters

**Recent sponsors include:**
- [Brand 1] (X% click rate, X conversions)

**Why my audience converts:**
1. They trust my recommendations (I only promote tools I use)
2. They're actively in "spending mode" (building side hustles = buying tools)
3. They've self-selected as builders (high buyer intent)

I'd love to send you a media kit with detailed analytics, audience demographics, and past sponsor results.

**Are you open to a 15-minute call next week to explore if this is a fit?**

You can also reply with questions or check out past issues at [YOUR URL].

Thanks for your time!

[Your Name]
Founder, AI Hustle Lab
[YOUR URL] | hello@aihustlelab.com

**P.S.** If now isn't the right time, no worries. Mind if I follow up in 3 months?

## Pricing Guidelines (Adjust Based on List Size)

| Subscribers | Solo Mention | Featured Mention | 3-Pack |
|---|---|---|---|
| 500-1,000 | $50-100 | $25-50 | $200-300 |
| 1,000-2,500 | $150-300 | $75-150 | $500-900 |
| 2,500-5,000 | $400-700 | $200-350 | $1,200-2,000 |
| 5,000-10,000 | $800-1,500 | $400-750 | $2,500-4,500 |
| 10,000+ | $1,500-3,000+ | $750-1,500+ | $4,500-9,000+ |

**Industry rule of thumb:** $20-40 CPM (cost per 1,000 subscribers in your list)

## Where to Find Sponsor Contacts

1. **LinkedIn:** Search "[Company] marketing manager" or "[Company] partnerships"
2. **Twitter:** DM founders directly (works surprisingly well for smaller brands)
3. **Email:** Find on company About page or through Hunter.io
4. **Referrals:** Ask your network if anyone knows marketing contacts
5. **Cold email tools:** Apollo.io, Lemlist (free tiers available)

---

## When to Start Pitching

- ✅ **500+ subscribers:** Start with small mentions ($25-50)
- ✅ **1,000+ subscribers:** Pitch solo sponsorships ($100-300)
- ✅ **2,500+ subscribers:** Premium pricing ($300-700)
- ✅ **5,000+ subscribers:** You can go full-time on this if you want

**Don't pitch before 500 subs** — brands expect at least 1,000+ for any meaningful partnership.

## Tracking Your Pitches

Create a simple spreadsheet with:
- Brand name
- Contact name + email
- Date pitched
- Follow-up date
- Response (yes/no/maybe)
- Deal terms if yes
- Revenue generated

Review monthly. Double down on what's working.
